By utilizing PyNIO, PyNGL, numpy, and xarray, this project successfully quantified the impact of climate change on Hurricane Sally's precipitation, providing compelling evidence that supports the innovative CAM5 technology.

PyNIO and PyNGL, powerful multi-format data I/O packages, was used to extract information from netCDF3 files by employing a multidimensional array module.

By leveraging the capabilities of numpy, a numerical computing tool, and Xarray, a module that enables the management of labels on arrays, spatial and temporal mappings, etc. I generated 2D visualizations involving a comparative analysis between IMERG and CAM5 data, showcasing the impact of climate change on precipitation for Hurricane Sally.

The findings revealed a staggering 61.5% increase in precipitation, providing further evidence in support of the innovative CAM5 technology.
